About Us

At Clever Angler, our journey began with a spark of inspiration from unexpected places. The clean lines of Frutiger Aero, the calming hue of green, and the whimsical vibes of Bubblegum by New Jeans all converged. But it was the gentle giant, the manatee, that embodied our spirit - serene, yet resilient. Our founder's passion for matcha and wellness merged with a vision to promote success, not just as a destination, but a mindful journey. Thus, our mission was born: to empower individuals to cultivate balance, clarity, and growth, one matcha-infused moment at a time.

Mission Statement

Steeped in excellence, fueling success one matcha at a time. Elevating minds and spirits through matcha-driven connections. Unlocking potential, sip by sip.

Vision Statement

To become the leading boba brand, inspiring global ambition. Connecting boba enthusiasts worldwide, fostering growth. Establishing boba as the ultimate motivational drink.
