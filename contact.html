FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

1.What services do you provide?

We offer a range of services including one-on-one counseling sessions, group therapy, workshops, and online resources designed to help individuals manage and overcome depression.

2. Who can benefit from your services?

Our services are designed for anyone experiencing symptoms of depression, whether it's mild feelings of sadness or more severe clinical depression. We welcome individuals from all walks of life.

3. How do I know if I need counseling?

If you find that feelings of sadness, hopelessness, or anxiety are affecting your daily life, relationships, or work, it may be beneficial to seek counseling. Our initial consultations can help determine the best course of action for you.

4. Do you offer both in-person and virtual sessions?

Yes, we provide both in-person counseling sessions at our office and virtual sessions via video call to accommodate your preferences and needs.

5. Are your services covered by insurance?

We work with various insurance providers. Please contact us directly to check if we accept your insurance plan or if we offer sliding scale payment options for those without insurance.

6. What should I expect during my first session?

During your first session, we will discuss your concerns and history. This is a safe space for you to express your thoughts, and we will work together to create a personalized plan for your journey toward healing.

What types of workshops do you offer?

We offer workshops focusing on coping strategies, mindfulness practices, stress management, and self-care techniques. These workshops are aimed at building resilience and providing practical tools for everyday life.

Is my information kept confidential?

Absolutely. We adhere to strict confidentiality guidelines to ensure your privacy and trust. All personal information shared with us remains secure.

What if I am in crisis?

If you are in immediate crisis or experiencing suicidal thoughts, please call emergency services or a crisis hotline in your area. Your safety is our top priority, and we encourage you to seek help immediately.
