Wall-E

WALL-E, a 2008 Pixar animated film, is set in a post-apocalyptic 2105 where humans abandoned Earth due to rampant consumerism and waste. WALL-E, a small waste-collecting robot, lives alone, compacting trash and dreaming of love. When EVE, a sleek search robot, arrives, WALL-E falls in love. EVE's mission is to find signs of life, and WALL-E's plant discovery prompts their journey to the human spaceship Axiom. There, they uncover the truth about Earth's downfall and humanity's reliance on automation. The film explores themes of environmentalism, loneliness and human connection.
